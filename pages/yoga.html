Beginner lessons

If you are looking for an energizing start for the day or a relaxing evening after a full day of adventures, our 1 hour yoga class is an experience not to be missed. All classes are beginner-friendly and suitable for all ages. All you need is some comfortable clothes and a water bottle!

The simple movement and breathing exersises will leave you feeling in harmony with body and mind, and allow a deeper sense of the surrounding mountains. During our I hour beginner yoga lesson, we are aiming for balance, flow and serenity, so you can take that harmonious feeling with you for the rest of the day. The classes contain gentle stretching and strengthening exercises combined with some easy breathwork and mindfulness.

Lessons are held every morning at 08.00am from thursday to sunday and should be booked in advance.

The perfect way to start or finish your days in the islands of the endless sunset!

The lesson begins in the Beach Bar, please meet us 10 minutes ahead of the lesson.
