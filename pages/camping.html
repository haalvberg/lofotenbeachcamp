CAMPING BY THE BEACH

TENTING WITH AN ENDLESS SUNSET

At Lofoten Beach Camp, you could tent right by the beach! Within the large designated areas, you are allowed to pick your own spot, whether you like to be close to facilities or the beach.

To make your experience more pleasent, we encourage all tenters to park your car in our designated parking lot. This is to ensure safety for our campers, protect our nature and avoid disturbance. At the parking lot we have 6 chargers for electric vehichles (extra charge) and trolleys to transport your luggage.

Tenter’s kitchen includes 5 cooking stations, while the beach bar welcomes you to charge your phones etc.

CARAVAN, MOTOR HOMES, VANLIFE BY THE BEACH

Being the biggest campsite in Lofoten and situated right on the beach, allows our guests to enjoy an excellent sea view with the famous midnight sun. We provide plenty of space for our guests arriving in caravans, motorhomes, and vans. The campsite has 75 electric spots, both 5A and 16A, which comes at at an additional price. We also offer plenty of spots without electricity.

You will find easy access to gray water and chemical waste disposal, as well as numerous drinking water taps.

No pre-bookings, first come, first serve.

During the high season, we recommend arriving before 18.00.

The Beach House

The Beach House contains 5 bedrooms, shared bathrooms, and a shared kitchen. We recently upgraded the common area for our guests, to put your focus towards showcasing the incredible view. Tall, new windows have been installed to ensure that our guests don’t miss a single minute of the Lofoten panorama.

At the Beach House, you could book either one or several of the rooms.

The backpacker-sized rooms have bunkbeds and come in 2 different sizes, either for 2 or 3 persons.

Due to the COVID-19 situation, the kitchen utilities are limited, but food can be prepared. Lofoten Beach Bar serves food and cold beverages during your stay.

Dogs/animals are not allowed in the Beach house.

Checkout before 11.00.

Free Wi-Fi at the campsite for all guests.

The Cabin

Our cosy cabin of 21 m² holds everything you need for a pleasant stay. Enjoy a lovely stay while the midnight sun shines through your window. The cabin comes with a fully equipped kitchen, bathroom and diningspace.

The cabin suits a familiy of 5, with a 3-person bunkbed and a 2-person futon.

Dogs/animals are not allowed in the Cabin.

Checkout before 11.00.

Free Wi-Fi at the campsite for all guests.
